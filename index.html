Masoud Khanalizadeh Imani

Computer Architecture & Storage Systems

I am a computer architecture researcher specializing in I/O and data storage systems. My recent work focuses on SSD/HDD caching strategies, reliability characterization of open-source caching modules, and cost-efficient performance optimization in large-scale SAN environments. Beyond theoretical design, I am passionate about building practical prototypes, conducting rigorous experimental evaluations, and developing tools that make advanced storage technologies more reliable and accessible. My broader goal is to contribute to the next generation of scalable, high-performance, and fault-tolerant storage infrastructures.
